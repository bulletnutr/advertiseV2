About Us

At Lost Fund Assistance, we specialize in helping people who have lost money online through scams, fraud, or failed transactions. Our mission is to restore hope and provide practical recovery solutions tailored to your unique situation.

Our consultants come from backgrounds in finance, law, and cybersecurity, combining their expertise to ensure that every client receives the best possible guidance.

We believe everyone deserves a second chance — and we’re here to walk beside you in your recovery journey.
